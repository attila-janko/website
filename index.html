About Terra Kontra

Terra Kontra is a collective of boundary pushing DJs and artists determined to shake the planet with underground sound. From warehouse raves to sun‑rise beach sets, we exist to amplify community, culture and raw energy.

Terra Kontra Festival

Every summer we host our own festival where the lineup is curated by the crew and the crowd. Expect 48 hours of non‑stop music, art installations and that indescribable TK vibe.
